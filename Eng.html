    WHAT IS VL'S FOOTBALL MATCH?

    Football matches that can be seen in Metaverse space are produced in AI and 3D.


    *AI NLP model learns football match records or online/console football game log data as training data to understand the flow of football.
    Through learning, the AI model can predict game scenarios when a game between two teams is played based on various data.

    AI NLP : Natural Language Processing model, Based on the existing text data, words or sentences are predicted within a given TASK.
    For example, there is a case in which AI, who learned the full volume of Harry Potter, wrote a new Harry Potter work.

    AI predicts game scenarios using input data such as
    the players' abilities of the two clubs and their recent performance.

    The scenario of predicted the game is delivered to the stunt team wearing motion capture equipment.
    And The stunt team creates a football animation based on the scenario.

    The final result is produced by creating a dramatic situation by partially adjusting motion data
    without departing from the scenario results predicted by AI.
    (like The rotation rate of the ball)

    The final soccer animation will be played in the Metaverse environment
    according to the announced date and time in advance.

    You can watch the game on an OTT platform
    such as Youtube or Twitch on the screen
    of the broadcast angle live-streaming video by the streamer.

    If you want to feel real and watch the match in the Metaverse,
    you can communicate with people around you and watch the game
    through VR devices or monitors.
